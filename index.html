Formula

V = L × [ W × T + Lw × Lh ] − Chamfer

The L-shape is decomposed into a flat slab (W × T) plus a vertical lip (Lw × Lh), extruded along L. A 45° chamfer on the top edge removes ½ × C² × L per chamfered edge.
